# Blacklight Web Designs: Branding & Design System

## 🎨 Palette: "The Midnight Spectrum"
*Explicitly No Purple, No Safe Blue.*

- **Onyx (Base):** `#0A0A0A` (Deepest black, grain texture added for depth)
- **Signal Lime (Accent 1):** `#D7FF00` (High visibility, technical feel)
- **Siren Red (Accent 2):** `#FF003C` (High contrast, urgency)
- **Spectral White (Text):** `#FDFDFD` (Pure contrast)

## 📐 Geometry: "Technical Sharpness"
- **Border Radius:** `0px` to `2px`. We explicitly reject the "Softness" of modern SaaS. Sharp edges equate to precision and high-value technicality.
- **Grids:** Asymmetric 90/10 layouts. We use negative space as a weapon to create tension.

## ✍️ Typography: "Command & Control"
- **Headlines:** *Clash Display* or *Space Grotesk* (Bold, Technical, High Impact)
- **Body:** *JetBrains Mono* or *Inter* (Mono for technical sections, Sans for elite readability)

## ✨ Motion Principles
- **Entrance:** Staggered Y-axis reveals with elastic easing.
- **Feedback:** Physical transformations. Buttons don't just change color; they "click" in 3D space (`scale`, `translate`).
- **Texture:** Persistent grain overlay on backgrounds to prevent "flatness."
