# Blacklight Web Designs: Client Onboarding Workflow

## Phase 1: The Socratic Intake
Every client must pass through our "Spectrum Audit."
- **Goal:** Identify the "Soul" and "Constraints."
- **Questions:**
  1. What is the one word that must define your brand's digital presence?
  2. Who is your most formidable competitor, and why do they bore you?
  3. What is the absolute deadline, and what is the cost of delay?

## Phase 2: Design Commitment
We present a **Design Commitment** document before a single line of code is written.
- **Components:** Radical style name, Topological hypothesis, Cliché liquidation list.
- **Approval:** Client must explicitly approve the "Risk Factor" before we proceed.

## Phase 3: The Build (4-Phase Workflow)
We follow the Antigravity Kit's standard:
1. **Analysis:** Deep dive into requirements.
2. **Planning:** Task breakdown and dependency mapping.
3. **Solutioning:** Architecture and high-fidelity design docs.
4. **Implementation:** Coding with real-time verification.

## Phase 4: The Maestro Audit
Before handover, we run the full `verify_all.py` suite.
- **Checks:** Security, UX Psychology (Miller's Law), Lighthouse (100/100 target), and E2E stability.

## Phase 5: Handover & Pulse Check
Deployment followed by a walkthrough of the custom design system and performance metrics.
