# Blacklight Web Designs: Lead Generation Strategy (SA SME Market)

Starting your hunt for business owners who need to "Reveal their brilliance."

## 1. The "Pulse Audit" Bait (Inbound)
- **Offer:** A free, 15-minute "Digital Health Check" or a R1,500 full Pulse Tier audit.
- **Strategy:** Share before/after performance metrics on LinkedIn and local Facebook business groups (e.g., "Entrepreneur Hub SA").
- **Hook:** "Is your website a slow-moving template? Let us reveal its unseen potential."

## 2. Niche Vertical Targeting (Outbound)
Focus on sectors with high growth but low design quality:
- **Local Specialized Retail:** Specialist coffee roasters, craft gin makers, boutique fashion.
- **Professional Services:** Independent consultants, lawyers, and architects who still use 2010-era sites.
- **Tech Startups:** Early-stage ventures in Cape Town/Jozi needing a "Nano Banana" edge to impress investors.

## 3. Collaborative Networking
- **SA Startup Incubators:** Reach out to local incubators to offer the "Spark Tier" as a launch package for new cohorts.
- **Brand Photographers:** Partner with local photographers; when they deliver high-end brand photos to a client, mention they need an elite site to match.

## 4. The "Spectrum Share" (Social Proof)
- **Consistency:** Post one "Technical Proof" or "Maestro Metric" every 48 hours.
- **Context:** Use the [Marketing Plan](marketing-plan.md) hooks to maintain a mysterious, elite brand voice.
